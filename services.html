Our Services

Precision iced cookie decorating for weddings, corporate events, and luxury gifting — royal icing detail, custom shortbread, and branded confectionery at scale

WHAT WE OFFER

Our Expertise

Royal Icing Detail Work

Precision flood icing, wet-on-wet layering, and multi-pass color separation — executed at a resolution no hand-piper can consistently match across a batch. Corporate logos, monograms, and portrait likenesses rendered on sugar cookies at 0.5mm detail. Food-safe gel colorants matched to Pantone references for brand accuracy.

Custom Shortbread & Sugar Cookies

Parametric cutter design lets us produce any silhouette — logos, maps, architectural elevations, monogram initials — in batch production quantities from 50 to 5,000 units. Scottish-style shortbread and classic sugar cookie bases available. Custom emboss dies add surface relief to uniced cookies for a premium tactile finish.

French Macarons & Sandwich Cookies

Macaron shells piped to consistent diameter with automated deposition for uniformity across large batches — critical for wedding favors and corporate gift sets where visual consistency matters. Custom ganache, buttercream, and jam filling systems. Color-matched to any palette. Gluten-free almond flour available on request.

Branded Gift Packaging

Custom cookie collections for corporate events, weddings, and retail gift sets. Branded boxes, ribbon-tied cellophane bags, and custom tissue inserts designed to your brief. Individually heat-sealed for 2-week ambient shelf life. Nationwide shipping available — packaging engineered to survive transit without crushing delicate iced surfaces.

OUR PROCESS

How We Work

1

Design Brief

Share your artwork, logo file, or concept reference. We'll advise on minimum feature resolution for icing — a 6px logo detail that prints fine in offset won't survive a 50mm cookie. We confirm dietary restrictions (nut-free, gluten-free, vegan) and minimum order quantities before quoting.

2

Cookie & Icing Specification

Choose your base (sugar cookie, shortbread, or macaron), icing finish (flood, wet-on-wet, hand-piped accent), and color targets. A digital proof of the final design is sent for approval before a single cookie is baked. Color-matching samples available for high-volume orders.

3

Bake, Print & Decorate

Cookies are baked fresh, cooled to specification, then decorated using precision icing deposition. Each batch is inspected against the approved proof before packaging. Hand-finishing adds accent details that automated deposition alone can't achieve — painted metallics, piped borders, hand-placed inclusions.

4

Packaging & Delivery

Cookies are individually heat-sealed or box-packed to your spec. Local Chicago-area orders can be hand-delivered day-of-event. Nationwide shipping via 2-day air with crush-resistant packaging. We recommend a 5-day lead minimum before your event date for standard orders; 10 days for large corporate runs.

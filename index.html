SPECIALTIES

Precision Cookie Decorating Services

01

Custom Cookie Toppers

Wedding monograms, portrait reliefs, company logos, and bespoke figurines printed in royal icing or white chocolate. Edible food color throughout — no surface paint — for long shelf life and safe consumption.

02

Sugar Sculptures

Architectural centerpieces, geometric statement pieces, and figurative icing sculptures at 1:1 scale or larger. Hollow construction for light weight. Sugar-glass translucency achievable in clear isomalt.

03

Chocolate Fine Detail

Tempered Belgian couverture printed in intricate filigree and relief patterns. Bar molds, bonbon shells, and showpiece elements with 0.4mm minimum feature. White, milk, and dark chocolate options.

EDIBLE MATERIALS

Food-Safe Printing Materials

Every material we print is 100% edible, food-safe, and produced in an allergen-aware facility. No plastics, no resins, no compromise.

  • ● Royal icing — clear to opaque, food-color throughout
  • ● Tempered dark, milk, and white chocolate
  • ● Colored fondant — Pantone-matched color targets
  • ● Marzipan — almond-based sculptural medium
  • ● Royal icing — detail work and structural joins
  • ● Wafer paper — ultra-thin edible sheet printing
